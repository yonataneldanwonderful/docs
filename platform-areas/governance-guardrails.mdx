## Governance & Guardrails

Governance & Guardrails provides controls to ensure agents behave safely, securely, and in compliance with your policies.

## Features

- **Content filtering** — Filter inappropriate content
- **PII protection** — Automatically detect and protect PII
- **Compliance controls** — Enforce compliance requirements
- **Access control** — Manage who can create and modify agents
- **Audit logging** — Track all changes and actions
- **Policy enforcement** — Enforce organizational policies

## Use Cases

Use Governance & Guardrails to:
- Protect sensitive information
- Ensure compliance
- Control agent behavior
- Audit agent actions
- Enforce policies

Governance & Guardrails ensures agents operate safely and securely.
