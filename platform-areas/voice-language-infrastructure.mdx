## Voice & Language Infrastructure

Voice & Language Infrastructure provides the underlying capabilities for voice interactions and multi-language support.

## Features

- **Text-to-Speech (TTS)** — High-quality voice synthesis
- **Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR)** — Accurate speech recognition
- **Voice selection** — Choose from multiple voices
- **Language support** — Support for multiple languages
- **Voice customization** — Customize voice characteristics
- **Low latency** — Optimized for real-time interactions

## Capabilities

- **Voice synthesis** — Natural-sounding speech
- **Speech recognition** — Accurate transcription
- **Multi-language** — Support for many languages
- **Voice cloning** — Custom voice creation
- **Emotion and style** — Expressive voice options

## Use Cases

Use Voice & Language Infrastructure for:
- Voice-based interactions
- Multi-language support
- Custom voice experiences
- High-quality audio
- Real-time conversations

Voice & Language Infrastructure powers natural, high-quality voice interactions.
