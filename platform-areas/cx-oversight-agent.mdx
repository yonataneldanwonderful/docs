## CX Oversight Agent

CX Oversight Agent provides AI-powered monitoring and oversight of customer experience across all interactions.

## Features

- **Quality monitoring** — Automatically assess interaction quality
- **Sentiment analysis** — Track customer sentiment
- **Issue detection** — Identify problematic interactions
- **Recommendations** — Suggest improvements
- **Trend analysis** — Track CX trends over time
- **Reporting** — Generate CX reports

## Capabilities

CX Oversight Agent can:
- Monitor interaction quality
- Detect customer dissatisfaction
- Identify improvement opportunities
- Track CX metrics
- Provide actionable insights

CX Oversight Agent helps ensure consistently high customer experience.
