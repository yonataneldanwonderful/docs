## WhatsApp Channel

The WhatsApp channel enables agents to interact with users through WhatsApp messaging.

## WhatsApp Capabilities

WhatsApp interactions support:
- **Messaging** — Send and receive WhatsApp messages
- **Rich media** — Images, videos, documents
- **Templates** — Pre-approved message templates
- **Quick replies** — Interactive button responses
- **Status updates** — Delivery and read receipts

## Configuration

Configure WhatsApp channels with:
- **WhatsApp Business API** — Connect to WhatsApp Business API
- **Phone number** — Register WhatsApp Business number
- **Templates** — Create and approve message templates
- **Media handling** — Configure media support
- **Compliance** — Set up compliance and opt-in

## Message Templates

WhatsApp requires templates for:
- **Initial messages** — First message to users
- **Promotional content** — Marketing messages
- **Transactional** — Order confirmations, updates

Templates must be approved by WhatsApp before use.

## Considerations

WhatsApp interactions have unique considerations:
- **Template approval** — Templates require approval
- **24-hour window** — Free-form messaging within 24 hours
- **Compliance** — Strict compliance requirements
- **Media limits** — File size and type restrictions
- **Opt-in** — Users must opt in to receive messages

## Best Practices

- **Get template approval** — Submit templates early
- **Respect 24-hour window** — Use templates after window
- **Handle media properly** — Support images and documents
- **Ensure compliance** — Follow WhatsApp policies
- **Test thoroughly** — Validate message delivery

WhatsApp enables messaging-based interactions through the popular messaging platform.
